A simple home for your notes, links, and ideas that stays accessible from your laptop and your iPhone anytime.

How to publish it

Free hosting

Use GitHub Pages or Netlify so the site works even when your desktop is off.

Mobile friendly

The layout already adapts nicely to smaller screens, so it feels good on iPhone.

Low maintenance

No server, no database, no install steps every day. Just open the link.

Fastest way to publish

  1. Create a free GitHub repo.
  2. Upload this folder to the repo.
  3. Turn on GitHub Pages in repo settings.
  4. Open the live URL on your phone and laptop.
